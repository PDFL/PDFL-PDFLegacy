Knowledge Graph

Knowledge graph that showing the citation relations of all the papers in the references section of your current paper. This can help to understand the relations between each work better.

Click on the knowledge graph button and you can:

  • Zoom-in zoom-out and move around the graph. On hover on one node you can see wich one is linked with it and paper info will be displayed with title, author and field of study

  • Change depth with the slider to see more correlated paper

  • Click on one node to expand just it and not the whole graph

  • On the bottom left you find a question mark, that on click, display color of field of study for a better understanding of correlated paper
